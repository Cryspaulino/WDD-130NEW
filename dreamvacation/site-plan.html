Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share information to help families from different countries so they can learn more about the Dominican Republic and create a dream vacation to enjoy our culture.

Audience

The intended audience are those families who dream about visiting the Dominican Republic but don't know where to start, starting with access to a mobile device, we bring them the opportunity to find ideas and create a plan.

Home page

Introduction You are welcome to DReam Vacation! Have you ever tried to find the perfect place to visit and felt that there was not much to see or many ideas for your vacation? Or even more, that you do not know how to make your kids enjoy and feel more involved in the vacation? If your answer to any of this is yes, you came to the right place! If not, we invite you to give it an eye and find the dream you did not know you were looking for.

Resources In this website, we provide you with general information about the Dominican Republic, ideas to plan a trip and to enjoy the best vacations in your life with your family.

More For more information on meals to try and activities to do with your kids, please visit the sections “Food” and “Kids” of our website.
